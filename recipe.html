Afrikana Recipe

Ugali, Kales & Meat Main Ingredients

2 Cups of Maize FLour

4 Cups of Water

4 Tomatoes

2 Onions

600 Grams of Kales

1 Kilogram of Meat

Ugali Procedure

Step 1

Add water to a cooking pot then wait for it to boil

Step 2

Add Flour to the boiled water

Step 3

Keep stirring till the mixture hardens

Step 4

Once you sense the smell of roasted mazie turn off the cooking pot and serve in a serving dish

Kales & Meat combo Procedure

Step 1

Add meat in a cooking and heat till water comes out and it starts to roast

Step 2

Add oinions, salt and cooking oil to the mixture and wait for 1 miunte

Step 3

Add tomatoes and stir for 2 minutes

Step 4

Add kales and water and wait for it to boil

Step 5

Stir the mixture for 3 minutes then serve
